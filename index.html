Welcome to Avgrund Maintenance Plugin Documentation.

This VqMod is the easiest way to add a little extra to your maintenance screen. We all hate to see this screen, as it means lost time selling, but its a fact of life that regular backup saves us in the event of a server meltdown.:

The base maintenance screen is a little generic and boring for today's graphical and effect driven web. Why spend money on a beautiful site and then show a plain boring maintenance message and screen to your users. These are potential sales that you are missing out on. So how to fix this?

Use the Avgrund Modal Plugin.

This plugin has been integrated into a VqMod with an administration module (which means you can control the details without having to code). All you need is a current version of VqMod which you can find here
